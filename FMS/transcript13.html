Personal Interview (PI)

Panelists: P1, P2 (Female), P3 (Male) (P3 left the room and did not return).

Me: Yes, ma'am. Gujarat State Fertilizers and Chemicals is a fertilizer and chemical company.

P2: Is it a state PSU?

Me: No, it is a private company promoted by the Gujarat government. (Started explaining how heavy regulation affects its status, but was interrupted.)

P2: We’ll get to that soon in your extempore.

Extempore

Topic: "Subsidy in the Fertilizer Sector"

Since I had recently read about this in the Economic Survey (2015-16), I tried to recall key points. However, my structure was poor, and my flow was chaotic. I jumped between points, forgetting key details.

  • Initially talked about the current cost-based subsidy system for **urea**, but then jumped to explaining **NPK-based methods**.
  • Realized I should cover all fertilizers, not just urea, so I started mentioning **DAP and MOP**, but time ran out before I could conclude.

Overall, **2/5 performance at best.**
